Afterlife Cinema

Afterlife Cinema takes place after all the main characters except the one in control of the time loop dies. They are put into a simulation so some form of them still live even though they are dead. However, a dangerous bug is found in the code, and the outcome of each loop worsens as they go.

The Aperture of a Swallowtail

The Aperture of a Swallowtail is a story about a man who is haunted by his childhood best friend, who became one of the Gods after the style of her death. When he decides to finally join her, they take residence in Shangri-La at border of Heaven and Hell. After a rule is broken, Shangri-La gets closer to hell.
